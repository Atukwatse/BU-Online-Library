About Our Library

Empowering academic excellence through innovative digital resources and exceptional service since 1948.

Our Mission

To support the teaching, learning, and research mission of Bugema University by providing access to quality information resources, fostering information literacy, and creating an environment conducive to academic excellence and intellectual growth.

Our Vision

To be a leading academic library in East Africa, recognized for excellence in digital innovation, comprehensive collections, and transformative services that empower students and faculty to achieve their full potential in a rapidly changing global environment.

Our History

A journey of growth and transformation in service to our academic community.

1948

Bugema University founded with a small collection of theological books.

1990

Expansion of library services to support new academic programs.

2010

Introduction of digital resources and online database subscriptions.

2020

Launch of comprehensive Online Library platform with 24/7 access.

2026

Modernized digital library serving over 10,000 students and faculty worldwide.
