Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

You can access the online library by logging into the login page using your student ID and password. Once logged in, you'll have access to all digital resources, e-books, and databases.
The physical library is open Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM, Saturday 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, and Sunday 1:00 PM - 6:00 PM. However, our online resources are available 24/7 for all registered students and faculty.
You can get research assistance through multiple channels: visit the reference desk during library hours, schedule a one-on-one consultation with a librarian, use our online chat service, or email us at library@bugema.ac.ug.
Yes! All registered students and faculty can access our digital resources from anywhere with an internet connection. Simply log in through the login page to access e-books, journals, and databases remotely.
If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot password?" link on the login page. Enter your student ID and registered email address, and we'll send you password reset instructions.
